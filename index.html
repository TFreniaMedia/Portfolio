Welcome

Thank you for showing enough interest in my work to come here and see it. The works that I have on this website are among the best works that I've made. I hope that you find something that you like among the works. Feel free to get into contact with me through my contact page. I will try to respond in a timely manor.

About

My name is Tyler Frenia (In case the title didn't give it away). I am 22 years old and I am currently studying Digital Media Production at SUNY Plattsburgh. I am also finishing a minor in Photography. Other hobbies that I am messing around with while I am here include, but are not limited to Animation and digital painting.

Before I enrolled here, I spent most of my life in persuit of the arts. I was trained in photography when I was only 3. My mother wanted someone to help her take photos of her dogs. So she trained my brother and I. We both still enjoy photography, but I spend much more time in the art side of photography.

About Me

As far back as I can remember, I have loved art. When I was little, that came out as a love for animated TV shows more then anything else on TV. As I grew older I started trying some of these different art forms my self. As one can expect, the photos and drawings I made around this time were less then good by any means. By the time that I was in 7th grade, I was already writing my first book and practicing drawing. I wish I had more from this time in my life, but it all disappeared with time.

The drawing to the left is the first art piece I did digitally.

What I do

So far in my life, I have taken the time to learn photography, cinematography, and animation to name a few. For the most part I work in photography and digital artworks. The digital artworks that I produce are mostly for personal use, but I am open to the idea of making paintings for a payment. I am much more open with photography. I really like doing photography so I will jump at any reason to get behind the camera.

Why Do I do What I do

There are many reasons for the work that I do. With photography, I take photos because I want people to be able to see the world through my eyes. I was once talking to a friend in California. I was talking about how boring it was were I lived. He was from the city, so he was curious what it looked like. His reaction to seeing something so familiar to me is one of the meny things that drives me. As for my artworks, I just want to get better at drawing so I keep pushing myself to do better and more complicated pieces.
